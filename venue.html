How to get to the conference

From the airport to the city

The "Marco Polo" Venice airport is on the mainland, around 5km north of Venice. The city can be easily reached by water bus, shuttle bus or taxi. More information available on the airport website.

Venice can also be reached easily from the "A. Canova" Treviso airport by bus. More information available on the airport website.

From Piazzale Roma or Santa Lucia train station

Palazzo Franchetti can be easily reached with water bus line 2, direction "San Marco": stop at "Accademia" and cross "Ponte dell'Accademia" (Accademia bridge).

Accomodation
